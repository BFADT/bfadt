In Parsons D+T we [use, remix, misuse, connect, work with] [emerging, new, everyday, obsolete, your] [technologies] to [critique, explore, investigate, examine, understand] and [create, build, destroy, replace, expand, shape] [our, your, the] [culture, society, world. the future, the present].

In Parsons Design + Technology, technology is the medium with which we investigate and shape our world.

We learn to create, employ, and think about emerging technologies and trends: Computers and Computation and Code; Embedded Processors and The Internet of Things; Augmented and Virtual Reality; Game Engines and Gamification; Social Media and Social Justice; Mobile Devices and Ubiquitous Computing; 3D Printing, Laser Cutting, Bitcoin Mining; Projection Mapping and Projected Futures; Drones and Surveillance; Machine Learning and Teaching through Play.

We use our understanding of technology, culture, and design thinking to solve new problems. We are Creative Technologists, Creative Coders, and Creative Directors; Game Designers, Game Developers and Game Artists; Experience Designers, Product Designers, and Interaction Designers.

We are Artists and Advocates.

Design the games you want to play, build the products you want to use, and create the future you want to live in.
